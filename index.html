About Us

"We React To What Matters"

Team Hermetica, was established as the departmental team of Chemical Engineering, at the National Institute of Technology, Hamirpur in the year 2014. "We react to what matters" to foster dexterous intelligence in the field of Chemical Engineering. We play a vital role in emerging & planned development to generate new knowledge by engaging in advanced research. We serve as a platform for both technical & non-technical ongoing ideas.
Team Hermetica undertake collaborative projects like a Stable salt reactor, Biofuel from Biowaste, Lithium-ion Battery, Membrane Technology, and many more. Which offers opportunities for long-term interaction with academics and industry.
Along with projects, we had captivating and amazing events such as Chemizzaro, Mind hunt, and Chem-e-switch, which challenge you at every step.
And organizing workshops and exhibitions that provide diverse opportunities for students to enlighten their inquisitive minds.

Vision

To build a vibrant environment to meet the expectations of technical human resources at the national level.

We explore new possibilities in chemical engineering coupled with research strengths in different new technologies, different polymers, biochemical and food engineering, etc. with a long-term vision of environmental protection.

Mission

To generate new knowledge by engaging in cutting edge research and undertake collaborative projects which offer opportunities for long-term interaction with academia and industry.

To train quality human and knowledge sources in the service of society. To develop sustainable products and technologies.
